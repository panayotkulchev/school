📖 Was / Were — Граматика

I/He/She/It → WAS
She was happy. It was cold.
You/We/They → WERE
They were at school.
Отрицание → WASN'T/WEREN'T
He wasn't late. We weren't home.
Въпрос → WAS/WERE + S?
Was she tired? Were you there?
WH → WH + was/were + S?
Where were you? Why was he late?
1
Попълни с was, were, wasn't или weren't
waswerewasn'tweren'tWasWere
2
Кратки отговори — Yes / No
Yes, I was.No, I wasn't.Yes, he was.No, she wasn't.Yes, they were.No, we weren't.

📖 Did / Didn't — Граматика

Утвърдително → глагол+ed
She played. They watched TV.
Отрицание → didn't + глагол
He didn't walk to school.
Въпрос → Did + S + глагол?
Did you listen to music?
Кратък → Yes/No + did/didn't
Did she cook? — Yes, she did.
WH → WH + did + S + глагол?
What did you watch?

📖 There is / There are — Граматика

Единствено → There is (There's)
There is a cat in the garden.
Множествено → There are
There are two books on the desk.
Отрицание → There isn't / There aren't
There isn't a park. There aren't any shops.
Въпрос → Is there / Are there?
Is there a bus stop? Are there any students?
Кратък → Yes, there is/are. No, there isn't/aren't.
Is there a pool? — Yes, there is.

📖 Can / Can't — Граматика

Умение → can + глагол
I can swim. She can play guitar.
Неумение → can't + глагол
He can't drive. They can't fly.
Въпрос → Can + S + глагол?
Can you cook? Can she dance?
Кратък отговор → Yes/No, S + can/can't
Can you swim? — Yes, I can.
Разрешение → Can I...? / You can / You can't
Can I go out? — Yes, you can.

📖 Prepositions of Place — Граматика

in — вътре в нещо
The cat is in the box. He's in the garden.
on — върху нещо
The book is on the table. She sat on the chair.
at — на определено място
I'm at school. She's at home. at the bus stop.
under — под нещо
The dog is under the bed.
next to — до / редом до
The shop is next to the park.
behind — зад / in front of — пред
The tree is behind the house.
between — между
She sat between Tom and Anna.
opposite — срещу
The bank is opposite the library.
